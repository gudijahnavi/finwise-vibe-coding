# Empathy Map — Persona: Ananya Rao (Online Shopper)

## SAYS
- "I just want to check out in a couple of taps."
- "Where's my order? I lost the tracking link."
- "Is this site even safe to enter my card details on?"

## THINKS
- "This site is asking too many questions before I can even pay."
- "I hope this product matches the photos."
- "I'll only come back if this is actually easy."

## DOES
- Compares prices across 2–3 tabs before buying.
- Abandons cart if checkout takes more than a couple of steps.
- Checks reviews/ratings before adding to cart.
- Re-visits the site just to check order status.

## FEELS
- **Frustrated** by slow, multi-step checkouts.
- **Anxious** about payment security on unfamiliar sites.
- **Relieved** when order tracking is clear and proactive.
- **Loyal** to sites that "just work" every time.

## PAINS
- Clunky/long checkout flow
- No visibility into order status after payment
- Poor search/filter making it hard to find the right product
- Account creation friction

## GAINS (what would delight her)
- One-click/guest + saved-address checkout
- Real-time order status (placed → packed → shipped → delivered)
- Clear, filterable product catalog with fast search
- Visible trust cues (secure payment badges, clear return policy)
