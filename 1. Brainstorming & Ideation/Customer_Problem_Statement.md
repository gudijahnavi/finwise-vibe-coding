# Customer Problem Statement

## Persona
**Name:** Ananya Rao
**Age:** 27
**Occupation:** Working professional in a mid-sized city
**Tech comfort:** Moderate — shops online regularly but is price- and time-conscious

## Situation
Ananya wants to buy everyday products (electronics accessories, apparel, home items)
online but doesn't have time to compare multiple sites, deal with clunky checkout flows,
or track scattered order confirmations across email and SMS.

## Problem
> "I want a single, fast, trustworthy place to browse products, check out securely, and
> track my order status — without creating friction at checkout or losing track of what
> I ordered and when it will arrive."

## What's blocking her
- Existing small e-commerce sites have slow, multi-page checkouts.
- No unified dashboard to view past orders and live status.
- Limited or unclear product search/filtering.
- Payment flow feels untrustworthy on lesser-known sites (no visible security cues).
- No easy way to manage addresses, wishlist, or repeat purchases.

## Why it matters
Cart abandonment and repeat-purchase drop-off are direct revenue losses for a growing
online store — and a poor account/order-tracking experience is one of the top cited
reasons customers don't return.

## SHOPEZ's answer
A clean, fast, full-stack storefront with: product catalog + search/filter, cart,
secure checkout, user auth, order history with status tracking, and an admin panel to
manage products/orders — solving the core friction points above.
